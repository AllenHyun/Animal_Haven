What is Animal Haven?

Animal Haven is a dedicated online platform designed to connect future pet owners with their perfect companions. Whether you are looking for a loyal dog, a playful cat, or a cheerful bird, our website makes the journey seamless. We proudly partner with local shelters across Spain to bring the entire experience right to your fingertips. Through Animal Haven, you can easily browse available animals, process all the necessary adoption paperwork online, and book in-person appointments to visit the shelters and meet your new best friend face-to-face.
